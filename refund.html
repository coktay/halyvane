Refund & Shipping Policy

HALYVANE LLC · Last updated: July 2026

Made-to-order & personalized items

Every Halyvane product is printed on demand and personalized to your specifications. Because each item is uniquely made for you, personalized products cannot be resold and are handled under the terms below.

Shipping

  • Processing time: orders are produced within 2–4 business days.
  • Shipping cost: a flat rate of $5.99 applies; shipping is free on orders over $50.
  • Taxes: applicable sales tax is calculated at checkout.
  • Tracked worldwide delivery is available. Delivery times vary by destination.

Returns & refunds

  • You may request a return or replacement within 30 days of delivery.
  • If an item arrives damaged, defective, or not as ordered (including an error in the personalization we produced), we will remake it or issue a full refund at no cost to you.
  • Because personalized items are made to order, we are unable to accept returns for buyer errors in personalization details (e.g. a misspelled name that was submitted correctly to us) or simple change of mind, unless the item is faulty.
  • Non-personalized items may be returned within 30 days in original, unused condition.

How to start a return

Email info@halyvane.com with your order number and a photo (for damaged or incorrect items). Our team will respond within 2 business days with next steps.
