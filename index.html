About Us

A small online retail brand with a made-to-order model.

How we operate and deliver our collections

Halyvane focuses on simple, giftable and everyday products that can be sold online without holding large amounts of physical stock at the beginning.

For printed apparel, the business may use a print-on-demand production model. This means products are produced after a customer order is placed, then fulfilled by a production or shipping partner where applicable.

Alongside apparel, Halyvane may also offer selected jewelry, accessories and small gift items through its upcoming Etsy shop and other online sales channels.

Note on Scale

Halyvane does not currently present itself as a large wholesaler or warehouse-based supplier. The current focus is online retail, marketplace sales and made-to-order products.

Order Process

How Orders Work

The process is designed for a small online retail business starting with marketplace sales.

01

Customer orders online

Once launched, customers browse products through the online store, view prices and place an order through the platform.

02

Item is prepared

Printed apparel may be produced after purchase using a print-on-demand partner. Other items may be handled according to current stock or supplier availability.

03

Order is dispatched

Shipping and delivery details will be provided through the sales channel after the order is processed.
