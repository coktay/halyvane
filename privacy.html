Privacy Policy

HALYVANE LLC · Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HALYVANE LLC ("Halyvane", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your information when you use our website and place an order.

Information we collect

  • Order information: your name, email address, shipping address and the personalization details you provide.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our third-party payment provider. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: basic, non-identifying information about how you use the site. Your shopping bag is stored locally in your browser.

How we use your information

  • To produce, fulfil and ship your orders.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your enquiries.
  • To send order confirmations and, where permitted, service updates.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate our store (such as payment processors, print and fulfilment partners, and shipping carriers). We do not sell your personal information.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@halyvane.com.
